TuringMachine

TURING is the native token of TuringMachine and can only be mined with farm/supply. There are no private or public sales of TURING.

The tokenomics (TURING) can be broken down as follows

  • 314159*2 = 628318 TURING hard cap.
  • 10% of all TURING mints will go to DevLockContract.
  • DevLockContract will lock the dev's TURING for 3 months from the first release of TuringMachine System.
  • After 3 months, DevLockContract will unlock 1/10 of the total dev's TURING each 30 days to use for development.
  • 0.05% of the stablecoin AMM trade fees are awarded to holders of the TURING token.
  • There are currently 0.05 TURING tokens minted per block, approximately 864 each day.
  • For the No-loss pools and farms, if you hold 20% your pool's balance as TURING token, you will get a bonus 1.2x of your mining speed.

Current deflationary mechanics

  • 5% profit on No-loss Pool used to buy TURING then burn
  • 0.5% profit on Farms used to buy TURING then burn

If you wish to participate in the discussion of our tokenomics then please visit our Github and create the issue.
