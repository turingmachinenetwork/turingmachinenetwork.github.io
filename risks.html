Risks of using Turing Network

Before making a deposit, it is best to research and understand the risks involved.

Smart contracts CANNOT be upgraded. This limits actions in a case of emergency, but leaves users fully in control of their funds.

Turing will be transitioning to a DAO to be fully decentralized.

When staking you use multiple smart contract products each of which has its own risks.

Audits

Turing smart contracts is open-source at our Github page, so we welcome all users to join us and participate/audit in our Smart Contracts.

However, security audits don't eliminate risks completely. Please don’t supply your life savings, or assets you can’t afford to lose.
