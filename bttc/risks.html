Risks of using Turing Network

Before making a deposit, it is best to research and understand the risks involved.

The owner CANNOT upgrade smart contracts. This limits actions in case of emergency but leaves users fully in control of their funds.

Turing will be transitioning to a DAO to be fully decentralized.

When staking, you use multiple smart contract products, each of which has its own risks.

Audits

Turing smart contracts are open-source on our Github page, so we welcome all users to join us and participate/audit our Smart Contracts.

However, security audits don't eliminate risks. Please don’t supply your life savings or assets you can’t afford to lose.
